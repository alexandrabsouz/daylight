Daylight

In Daylight, the singer talks about a very paradoxical feeling, which is the intense desire for something that we know will hurt us.

For this, David Kushner said in interviews that he used some biblical images that are paradoxical, such as the contrast between light and dark, light and darkness.

"There's darkness in the distance From the way that I've been livin' but I know I can't resist it."

About David Kushner

David Alan Kushner II is an American singer and songwriter. Raised in the suburbs of Chicago, Illinois, he moved to Los Angeles, California and began releasing music.

Song Curiosities

  • David is the youngest of six children
  • He taught himself to play guitar with the hits of the band Green Day
  • His older brother had a band
